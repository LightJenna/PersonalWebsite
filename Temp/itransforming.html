Example:

You Will Need

  • Two (2) pieces of origami paper
  • Scissors

Step 1

Take one of the pieces of paper. Fold in half (match edge to edge). Crease.

Step 2

Unfold and rotate 90 degrees. Fold in half again the same way, so that your paper is folded like +.

Step 3

Cut along the + lines, so you have 4 smaller squares, all the same size.

Step 4

Take one of the small squares. Fold in half (side to side, like before).

Step 5

Unfold and rotate 90 degrees. Fold in half again the same way, so that your paper is folded like +.

Step 6

Fold in half by matching up the corners. Unfold.

Step 7

Do the same the other direction. That should give you 8 segments. + and x

Step 8

Fold a corner into to center. It should line up with your prior + folds. Fold the corner next to it as well.

Step 9

Flip it over. Then pinch the + fold where you folded the corners in.

Step 10

Push the fold inward, like shown. You should have something like the Step 11 pic.

Step 11

Repeat steps 4-10 with the other 3 small squares. Repeat steps 1-11a for the other piece of origami paper.

Step 12

Take one from each pile if they are different colors. If they are the same color, it doesnâ€™t matter. Put them together and fold one of the poking out corners down. Do the same for the other.

Step 13

Add another one on. When you get all of them connected on, carefully bend your disk like shape so that the ends fit together.

Step 14

Fold the corner between the pieces. Do the same for the other corner.

Step 15

Gently work it inward, one direction at a time. It may take a while to get it to go smoothly, depending on how slick your paper is. (slicker=better)

You're Done!
